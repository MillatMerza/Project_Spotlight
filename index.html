Spotlight FAQ

Answers to common questions about AR experiences

Whether you're new to augmented reality or looking to enhance your current workflow, find everything you need to know about creating and managing AR content with Spotlight.

What devices support AR viewing?

→

Spotlight works on iOS and Android devices with ARCore/ARKit support. No special apps required - works directly in mobile browsers.

How do I upload 3D models?

→

Use our web dashboard or API to upload GLB/GLTF files. We automatically optimize models for AR viewing.

Can I try before subscribing?

→

Yes! Start with our free tier offering 5 AR experiences and basic analytics.

What about data privacy?

→

All model data is encrypted at rest and in transit. We comply with GDPR and CCPA regulations.
